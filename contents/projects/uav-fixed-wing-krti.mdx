## Performance & Speed
One of the standout features of this aircraft is its propulsion-to-drag optimization, allowing it to reach a **Top Speed of 64 m/s (approx. 230 km/h)**. Maintaining structural integrity and autonomous control at this velocity was the primary engineering challenge.

## Technical Specifications (KRTI 2025 Standards)
* **Speed Capability**: Max speed of 230.4 km/h with stable autonomous cruising.
* **Aerodynamics**: Low-drag airframe design with a wingspan of 125 cm to meet minimal lift requirements while maximizing forward thrust.
* **Payload**: Successfully carries the mandatory 1 kg payload (4x 250ml containers) without significant performance degradation.
* **Avionics**: Integrated with **Mission Planner** for precise waypoint navigation and real-time telemetry at high speeds.

## Key Challenges
Managing the **Aerodynamics** at 64 m/s required precise tuning of the PID controllers in ArduPilot to prevent oscillations. The result is a racing-grade UAV capable of completing the mission circuit with industry-leading lap times.
